Roof Tiles Generator

Geometry Nodes modifier to generate realistic roof tiles

Create high quality realistic roofs

Generate mesh roof tiles for a highly realistic roof appearance.

Roof Tiles Generator types

Flat Rectangle

Generates fully procedural geometry of rectangular tiles. Allows you to control the tile shape directly through modifier parameters.

Custom Tile

Allows you to use a custom tile mesh and distribute it across the roof surface, giving you the flexibility to create your own unique tiles.

Asset-Browser Ready

Simply drag & drop a roof preset from the Asset Browser for instant setup.

MultiTexture Material

The specially developed MultiTexture Node lets you add up to 16 textures to each shader input, automatically distributing them randomly across the roof tiles for natural variation. [SHADER SAMPLE]

Seamless Material

This method uses one seamless texture. The Roof Tiles Generator unwraps each tile to a random position in the UV space, ensuring each tile has a unique, non-repeating look. [SHADER SAMPLE]
